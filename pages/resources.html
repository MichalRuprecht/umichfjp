Resources for Flint ResidentsBETA

This website includes a list of resources for Flint residents. The website was created by the Flint Justice Partnership, a student-run organization at the University of Michigan.
The list below provides an easy-to-read and concise list. To find more resources in your area, use the State of Michigan help hotline. Dial 211 or click here.

Food

  • Food Bank of Eastern Michigan
    This resource assists hungry individuals and families in Flint and surrounding areas. Click here to find the closest food pantry and community kitchen in your area. You could also download the Got Food? app on Google Play or the App Store to find the closest locations.
    SNAP is the federal food assistance program and the first line of defense against hunger. Click here to get assistance to apply for SNAP benefits.
    Food giveaways will start at 10 AM every Tuesday at the Salvation Army (1475 W Coldwater Rd). Click here to find youth feeding sites.

Resources for Flint Residents

This website includes a list of resources for Flint residents. The website was created by the Flint Justice Partnership, a student-run organization at the University of Michigan. The list below provides an easy-to-read and concise list. To find more resources in your area, use the State of Michigan help hotline. Dial 211 or click here. If you have any suggestions, questions, comments, and/or concerns, click here to email the Flint Justice Partnership.
The sticker shows resources avaliable during the COVID-19 pandemic.

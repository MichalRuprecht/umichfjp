Resources for Flint Residents

This website includes a list of resources for Flint residents. The website was created by the Flint Justice Partnership, a student-run organization at the University of Michigan. The list below provides a concise list of resources. To find more resources in your area, use the State of Michigan help hotline. Dial 211 or click here. If you have any suggestions, questions, comments, and/or concerns, click here to email the Flint Justice Partnership.
The sticker shows resources avaliable during the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • Hamilton Community Health Network
    Hamilton dental services provide everyone with care. Click here or call to schedule an appointment.
    No new resources added.

  • Mott Children’s Health Center
    The Child and Adolescent Dentistry Department at Mott Children’s Health Center provides dental services for eligible Genesee County kids up to age 18. Call to schedule an appointment.
